Our Story
Home About Us

Promise seeks to make the Bible easy to understand and apply to your life.

We are a real church with real people who want to share the Good News of Salvation through Jesus Christ. We make mistakes, but we try to be better. We don't look down on others because they've had a hard time or don't know their Bible. We believe that everyone is welcome at our church, no matter what their background or beliefs.

Vision & Mission

Our mission is to take the living and active Word of God to all people, bringing them grace and salvation, and making them workers in the kingdom of God.
