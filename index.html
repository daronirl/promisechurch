Welcome to Promise Church

Chris was born and raised in Baltimore, Maryland. He grew up in a row home in a poor part of the city. It was a violent time, with desegregation, busing, riots, and racial tensions warranting the National Guard. His father was a printer and his mother was a stay at home mom who could make a meal that would feed a family of four for a week.

Sunday Services

We offer three worship services every Sunday morning.

Pastor Chris Deknatel delivers the same great message at all three services. Bringing the Word of God, Living, and Active to all people.

  • 8:00am Traditonal

    Organ led hymns and traditional liturgy are the highlights of our Traditional service.

  • 9:30am and 11:00am

    Our modern worship band leads the music at our contemporary services.

  • Live Stream Services

    You can find us on Facebook, YouTube, and Vimeo every Sunday. We also have an archive of all our past messages on our Vimeo page.
